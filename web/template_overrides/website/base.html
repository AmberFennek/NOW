Planned Features of NOW

  • Being able to tag anything in the virtual world, much like you do with image sharing sites.
  • Flags system is extensible, not limited to the one alphabet letter, but any single word.
  • A three dimensional (3D) simplification of movement within a 4x4x8 grid of room regions.
  • Achievement system - awards based, encourages learning new things and regular usage and improvement by and for players.
  • Provide commands that users are familiar with.
  • Provides a familiarity for folks that are not ready to dive in to it via a portal to "The Past".
  • As rooms are maintained and updated they are promoted to the NOW, if desired.
  • Primarily a Resource Based Economy with a monetary "helper" system.
