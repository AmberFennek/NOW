Welcome to NOW! 😘 😝 😢 😇 😆 😈 😏 😓 😍 😖 😎

NOW stands for "Novel Online World" and is the prototype for future text based worlds. The framework uses Evennia as the server. It will allow for a world that is in some ways simpler than a TinyMU, allowing you to chat in one channel OOCly with your account name, yet allow you to pursue your own story ICly with one or more characters. Your story might be very MUD-like ߝ your character versus mobs, puzzles, and environment ߪ or MUSH-like, with RP or scenes with characters.
