Welcome to NOW! 😘 😝 😢 😇 😆 😈 😏 😓 😍 😖 😎

NOW stands for "Novel Online World" and is the prototype for future text based worlds. The framework uses Evennia as the server. It will allow for a world that is in some ways simpler than FurryMUCK, meaning you can sit in one channel and chat OOCly as a player, yet allow you to pursue your own story ICly with one or more characters. Your story might be very MUD-like ߝ your character versus mobs, puzzles, and environment ߪ or MUSH-like, with RP or scenes with characters.
